GSSD Golf Swing Sway Detection Help Page

Welcome to the GSSD Golf Swing Sway Detection System!

The GSSD is designed to help you improve your golf swing by detecting any sway movements. Follow the simple steps below to set up and use the device effectively.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Step 1: Allow Camera Access

    When prompted, make sure to allow the device to access your camera. This step is crucial for the system to monitor your movements accurately.

  • Step 2: Position Yourself

    Stand on one of the sides of the white box displayed on the screen. Ensure that you are completely visible within the frame of the camera.

  • Step 3: Prepare to Swing

    Get into your golf stance and prepare to make your swing. Make sure you are comfortable and properly aligned within the white box.

  • Step 4: Swing

    Execute your golf swing as you normally would. Focus on maintaining your form and staying within the boundaries of the white box.

  • Step 5: Monitor for Beeps

    If you move out of the white box during your swing, the device will emit a beeping sound. The beep indicates that you have swayed, and your form needs adjustment.

Tips for Best Results

  • Lighting

    Ensure the area is well-lit so the camera can capture clear images.

  • Background

    Avoid cluttered or overly busy backgrounds for better detection accuracy.

  • Practice

    Regular use of the GSSD can help you identify and correct sway in your swing.

By following these steps and tips, you'll be on your way to achieving a more stable and effective golf swing. Happy golfing!
